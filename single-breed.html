What are the basic facts of an Affenpinscher?

Affenpinscher is a combination of two German words ‘affen’ and ‘pinscher’. ‘Affen’ refers to monkey and ‘pinscher’ means terrier. To put it into perspective, they have a characteristic monkey like face. Thus, the name Affenpinscher denotes the breed. Affenpinschers are territorial by nature. They fiercely protect their belongings and surroundings. So, they usually disprove of any trespassing. It’s tiny body comparatively contains a big dog attitude.
